Lasagna

Description

A delicious italian dish of layered pasta sheets, sauce, cheese, and meat.

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sheets
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Ground Beer or Pork

Steps

  1. Place all the ingredients in an oven safe dish
  2. Place in a pre-heated oven at 460 degrees
  3. Bake until its done.
